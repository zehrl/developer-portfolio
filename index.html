Bio

Hi, my name is Logan Zehr and I'm an aspiring Web Designer. My background is quite diverse and interesting for this field. I'm originally from Fort Wayne, Indiana where I grew up in the country. After finding my passion for creating things, I decided to pursue my BS in Mechanical Engineering at Purdue University. Fast forward to today, I'm now located in Seattle, Washington where I've held a couple of engineering positions in Manufacturing from Design to Quality Assurance. In my free time, I love producing electronic music and DJ'ing.
